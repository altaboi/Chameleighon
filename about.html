About Leigh

Chameleighon — Specialists in spray painting for the rooms that matter most

Some things in a home should be replaced when they wear out. And some things only look like they need replacing.

Chameleighon was built on a simple belief: that a kitchen, bathroom or bedroom shouldn't need a full renovation just because it looks dated. In most cases, the underlying structure is perfectly sound — what's changed is the finish. The colour. The surface.

Leigh Ehret founded Chameleighon — a name that blends her own name with the chameleon's gift for transformation and the lion's strength. Based in Stellenbosch, Leigh brings a personal, expert approach to every project across the Western Cape.

We use professional spray painting techniques to give those surfaces a new lease of life. The same equipment used by furniture manufacturers. The same quality of finish. Applied in your home, by a specialist who takes preparation as seriously as painting.

Every project begins with a thorough home visit and honest advice. Leigh will tell you exactly what's achievable, what to expect and what it will cost — before a single piece of masking tape goes down.

Our values

Preparation first

A lasting finish starts before the spray gun comes out. Every surface is cleaned, sanded and primed correctly.

Honest pricing

Every quote is clear, itemised and fixed. No surprises on completion.

The right materials

Premium, professional-grade paints and coatings selected for each surface and environment.

Personal service

Leigh handles every consultation personally. You deal with the expert from start to finish.

We don't leave until it's right

Every project ends with a full walkthrough. If it's not perfect, we make it right.
