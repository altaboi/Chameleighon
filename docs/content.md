### General Questions

**Q: What is professional spray painting for kitchens and bathrooms?**

Professional spray painting uses specialist spray equipment to apply primer and finish coats to kitchen cabinets, bathroom vanities and bedroom furniture. The result is a smooth, factory-quality finish that is more durable and more even than anything achievable with a brush or roller. Chameleighon, based in Stellenbosch, delivers this service across Cape Town and the Western Cape.

---

**Q: Is spray painting kitchen cabinets worth it?**

Yes. Professional kitchen spray painting delivers a transformation that looks equivalent to a full kitchen replacement, at a fraction of the cost — typically saving 70–80% compared to buying new cabinets. The finish is durable, moisture-resistant and available in any colour. For structurally sound cabinets that simply look dated, spray painting is the most cost-effective upgrade available in South Africa.

---

**Q: How long does a spray-painted kitchen finish last?**

A professionally spray-painted kitchen finish, properly prepared and applied with premium coatings, typically lasts 8–15 years with normal use. Chameleighon uses professional-grade moisture and grease-resistant coatings on all kitchen projects to ensure lasting results.

---

**Q: How long does kitchen spray painting take?**

Most Chameleighon kitchen spray painting projects are completed in 1–3 days, with a full turnaround of 3–5 days including curing time. Full cure before normal use is 24–72 hours after the final coat. Most homeowners in Stellenbosch and Cape Town have their kitchen back in use within the same week.

---

**Q: How much does it cost to spray paint kitchen cabinets in South Africa?**

A typical kitchen spray painting project with Chameleighon ranges from R10,000 to R50,000 depending on kitchen size, number of doors and surface condition. This compares to R150,000–R250,000+ for a full kitchen replacement. Leigh provides a clear, itemised quote after a free home visit — WhatsApp 082 330 8900 to get started.

---

**Q: Do I need to empty my kitchen before spray painting?**

You do not need to empty your entire kitchen. Remove small countertop appliances such as kettles and toasters beforehand. Leigh's team handles all professional preparation, masking and protection of countertops, floors and cabinet interiors. You simply clear the surfaces — Chameleighon does the rest.

---

**Q: What surfaces can be spray painted in a kitchen?**

Professional kitchen spray painting can be applied to cabinet doors, drawer fronts, cabinet frames, end panels, kitchen islands, plinths and architrave. Both wood and MDF surfaces are suitable. The service is not suitable for worktops, tiled surfaces or kitchen appliances.

---

**Q: Can bathroom furniture be spray painted?**

Yes. Bathroom vanities, cabinet units, bath panels and shelving can all be professionally spray painted. Chameleighon uses specialist primers and moisture-resistant topcoats formulated for high-humidity environments, ensuring the finish remains durable and does not peel or blister in a bathroom setting.

---

**Q: What is the difference between spray painting and brush painting for cabinets?**

Spray painting applies paint as a fine mist, producing a smooth, even finish with no brush marks or roller texture. For kitchens and bathrooms where surfaces are examined closely and touched frequently, a sprayed finish is more durable, more consistent and more professional. It is the method used by furniture manufacturers — applied in your home by Leigh.

---

**Q: Does spray painting add value to your home?**

A freshly spray-painted kitchen or bathroom is one of the most cost-effective ways to increase the perceived value of a home before sale. Estate agents in the Western Cape consistently identify kitchens and bathrooms as the rooms buyers inspect most closely. Chameleighon's spray painting service offers a strong return on investment for homeowners preparing to sell.

---

**Q: What colours are available for spray painting?**

Spray painting can be done in virtually any colour. Chameleighon offers a wide range from leading paint manufacturers and can colour-match to specific RAL, Farrow & Ball, Little Greene or any other reference. A free colour consultation with Leigh is included with every quote. Message on WhatsApp to start the conversation.
